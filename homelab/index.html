Welcome to Moist Haus!


Moist Haus is the name of my homelab. It is my polymorphic sandbox, test bench, production environment and digital workshop. After college it became a major part of my life that has blended my desire to be a holistic engineer, to continue to learn and to centralize my personal computing.
